## DX Is Not as Hard as You Think

When you hear "DX (Digital Transformation)," you might imagine large-scale system implementations and expensive investments. But for small businesses in Miyako Island, DX can be much simpler and more approachable.

## 3 DX Steps You Can Start Today

### 1. Introduce Cashless Payments

Many tourists prefer credit cards or QR code payments. Services like Square or Airpay have low initial costs and are easy to set up.

### 2. Digitize Reservation Management

Switch from paper notebooks to Google Calendar or online booking systems. This prevents double bookings and enables automatic reminders.

### 3. Start Sharing on Social Media

Use Instagram or LINE Official Account to regularly share updates about your business. In Miyako Island, beautiful scenery and food photos generate high engagement.

## Start Small

You don't need to change everything at once. Pick the one thing that will have the biggest impact and start there.
