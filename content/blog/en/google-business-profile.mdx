## Why Google Business Profile Matters

Most customers search Google for local businesses before visiting. By optimizing your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business), you can appear prominently in these search results and attract more foot traffic.

## Key Setup Tips

### 1. Enter Accurate Information

- Business name (official name)
- Address (pin the correct location on Google Maps)
- Business hours (reflect seasonal changes)
- Phone number
- Website URL

### 2. Add Quality Photos

- Exterior photos (help first-time visitors find you)
- Interior photos (convey the atmosphere)
- Product/menu photos
- Staff photos (add a personal touch)

### 3. Use the Posts Feature

Post updates about events, specials, or news 1-2 times per week. Regular updates positively impact your Google ranking.

## Managing Reviews

- Reply to every review politely
- Address negative reviews honestly and professionally
- Ask satisfied customers to leave reviews
