## Introduction

LINE Official Account is one of the most accessible and effective digital marketing tools for small businesses. Since most of your customers already use LINE daily, you can expect much higher open rates compared to email.

## What is LINE Official Account?

LINE Official Account is a business-oriented LINE account that lets you broadcast messages, issue coupons, manage reservations, and chat with customers who add you as a friend.

### Key Features

- **Message Broadcasting**: Send promotions and updates to all followers at once
- **Digital Coupons**: Create coupons to encourage repeat visits
- **Rich Menu**: Customize interactive menus with buttons for booking, ordering, etc.
- **1-on-1 Chat**: Handle individual customer inquiries directly

## Setup Steps

1. Visit [LINE Official Account](https://www.linebiz.com/jp/) website
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Fill in your business information
4. Set up your profile (shop name, photo, business hours)
5. Generate your QR code for friend additions

## Use Cases for Small Businesses

- **Diving Shops**: Automate booking confirmations and weather updates
- **Restaurants**: Share daily specials with photos
- **Hotels & Guesthouses**: Pre-check-in information and sightseeing guides
