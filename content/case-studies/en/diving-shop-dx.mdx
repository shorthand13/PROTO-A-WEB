## Challenge

A popular local diving shop was managing bookings with paper ledgers and notes. During peak season, information sharing between staff couldn't keep up, resulting in 2-3 double bookings per month. The shop also couldn't handle after-hours bookings or international inquiries effectively.

## Solution

Proto-A implemented improvements in three steps:

1. **LINE Official Account Setup**: Centralized booking through LINE with auto-reply messages enabling 24/7 reservation acceptance.
2. **Google Calendar Integration**: LINE bookings automatically sync to Google Calendar, giving all staff real-time visibility into the schedule.
3. **Multilingual Support**: Set up the LINE rich menu in both Japanese and English.

## Results

- **Double bookings**: From 2-3/month to **zero**
- **Reservations**: **30% increase** within 3 months
- **International bookings**: **Doubled**
- **Staff time on booking management**: **Reduced by 2 hours/day**

> "Since implementing LINE bookings, the quality of our customer service has improved dramatically. International bookings have also increased — we're truly grateful." — Shop Owner
