## Challenge

A local guesthouse had been operating with phone-only reservations since opening. When the owner couple was busy with guests, they couldn't answer calls, leading to missed bookings. Younger customers and international travelers tend to avoid phone bookings, so potential guests were being lost.

## Solution

1. **Online Booking System**: A simple, user-friendly booking form allowing smartphone reservations in just a few taps.
2. **Google Business Profile Optimization**: Direct links from search results to the booking page, with enhanced photos and reviews to build trust.
3. **Automated Confirmations**: Automatic confirmation messages to guests upon booking, with LINE notifications to the owner.

## Results

- **Monthly bookings**: **40% increase**
- **Phone handling time**: **Reduced by 3 hours/day**
- **International bookings**: From zero to **10+ per month**
- **Review rating**: 4.2 → **4.7** (Google Reviews)
